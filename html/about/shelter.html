The Animal Shelter

Saving one animal will not change the world, but the whole world will change for that animal

The PetRescue was founded in 2001 by Anna Caldwin with the aim to help and support animals in need

Since the shelter first opend in 2001 we have hellped over 1000 animals to find new homes

The Shelter Guidlines

  • Every animal should have a safe and protected space were they feel comfortable
  • Every animal needs medical care to ensure a pain free and happy existance
  • Every animal should have acces to water at all times and food should be given at regular intervals
  • Every animal should get the mental and physical stimulation that they need

As of today we have 10 animals at our shelter, but new animals are constantly arriving. To take care of all our animals is not an easy task and witout the help from outside support we would not be able to keep our shelter running.
