Foster Home

Sometimes we receive more animals than we have space for. In order to make sure that no animal is left behind duting these times we rely on foster homes.

What is a foster home?

Foster homes are families that accept to take care of an animal untill we find a suitable home.

A foster home is a place where the animal should feel at home and be taken care of like a real member of the family.

Why do we need foster homes?

Sometimes the shelter is overflowing, in these cases we need to place our animals in foster homes.

Foster families ensures that all our animals are given the care that they need, even during stressfull times.

What is required to be a foster home?

Giving an animal away is not something that we do lightly, even if it is just for a short time. Therefore, it is important for us to ensure that each foster family fullfills certain requirements.

As a foster home you need to:

  • Have relevant knowlege about the type of animal that you will be taking care of.
  • Have the time needed to take proper care of the animal.
  • Be willing to invest in food and general equipment that the animal needs.
