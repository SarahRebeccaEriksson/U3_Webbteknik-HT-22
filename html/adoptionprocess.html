The Adoption Process

The best way that you can help us is to help one of our animals. When you addopt an animal you accept a new family member into yout home.

Why does it cost to adopt?

The price for the adoption serves two purposes, firstly it covers the veterinary costs related to the adoption such as medical checks and chip placement, secondly it serves as a barriere against people who just want a free animal without a serious attitude

Prices for adoption

  • Dogs: 400 Euro
  • Cats: 350 Eruo
  • Small Animals: 200-300 Eruo

Requirements

We are extremly carefull to make sure that our animals end up in good homes. Therefore we require some things from you.

  • Accept a home visit before the adoption
  • Proof that you have time to take care of the animal
  • Proof of a stable income to cover food and medical costs for the animal
  • Have experience in animal care or be willing to learn
  • Agree on a visit 6 months after the adoption

The Contract

In order to ensure that the requirements are fullfilled, each adopter will need to agree to the terms by signing our adoption contract.
