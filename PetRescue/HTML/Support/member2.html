Membership

One of our main sources of income comes from our trusty members. The support from you helps us tremendously

What does it mean to be a member?

As a member you support our work in more than one way. The economical contribution helps with our medical bills and care taking costs, but you also help us on a political level as you become a part of our partitionings toward better animal care.

What will you get as a member?

As a member you do not only support us, you also get exclusive acces to our weekly letters and news

Furthermore, all members are invited to our anual meetings were your ideas can come to life
